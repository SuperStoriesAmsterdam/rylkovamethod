Seattle · the mechanism

She stayed for the anatomy.

Brian’s school, 2011. The magic was suddenly explained in anatomical terms — and never stopped being beautiful. “The magic became mechanism.”

The story

It started as a vacation. It became a method.

Yulia trained in Chiang Mai, then went to Seattle for a massage license so she could practise Thai work legally — and discovered myofascial anatomy. With Brian she built her signature: the stretch and flow of Thai bodywork brought onto the treatment table, so therapists deliver it without crawling the floor, and without wrecking their own bodies.
